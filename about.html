About HOT & COOL Cafe

Hot + Cool Cafe, a Los Angeles County-certified social enterprise, is a local
Black-owned and family-operated coffee shop in the heart of
Leimert Park Village that opened in 2018.

The Cafe is committed to making sure all their neighbors have access
to healthy food, regardless of circumstance.

Owners Tony Jolly and Tina Amin
intentionally chose Leimert Park
as the location for their vegan cafe
in what has historically been a food desert
for convenient, healthy food choices
in South Central.

Social Impact

Hot + Cool served as a 2020 Voting location, providing voters with a safe, neighborhood location to engage in our democratic process.

Hot + Cool Cafe utilizes their venue to share music, tackle social justice issues, and discuss ways to improve the quality of life for residents of Leimert Park.

Hot + Cool tackles food inequity
in South LA through their
Senior Meal Program.

Owner Anthony Jolly and the cafe have been recognized by Governor Newsom and Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti. Jolly turned Hot + Cool into a community kitchen providing thousands of meals to senior citizens and those negatively economically impacted by COVID-19.

Hot + Cool tackles food inequity by partnering with LA Community Fridges, including the Leimert Park Community fridge).

As a certified social enterprise, Hot + Cool Cafe’s mission
is to ensure that South Central communities have access to Black-centered arts and culture, delicious, plant-based food, and employment opportunities
and training, regardless of circumstance.

The core of their business thrives on community building and togetherness.

Hot + Cool proudly serves coffee carefully selected
and roasted by its founders, as well as hand-blended teas.

People from all walks of life come together at Hot + Cool Cafe to enjoy delicious food, coffee, music, and conversation.

COMMUNITY INVESTMENT

In 2020, Hot + Cool Cafe partnered with local nonprofit MISLA- Made In South LA, which trains historically underserved youth for high-level careers in tech and digital marketing while they simultaneously work on real-world projects. The Cafe hired their design and creative agency MARTY (Making A Reality That’s Yours), to engineer their new website and marketing campaigns.
